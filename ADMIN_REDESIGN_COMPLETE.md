## Design System

### Color Palette
- **Background**: Gradient from slate-900 to slate-800
- **Primary**: Dynamic primary color (CSS variable)
- **Accents**: White with 10-40% opacity for glassmorphism
- **Text**: White with varying opacity levels
- **Status**: Green (success), Red (error), Blue (info)

### Typography
- **Headings**: Font-black (900 weight) with tracking
- **Body**: Font-bold with proper hierarchy
- **Labels**: Font-bold uppercase with tracking

### Components
- **Cards**: Glassmorphic with backdrop blur and borders
- **Buttons**: Gradient backgrounds with hover effects
- **Inputs**: Glass effect with focus states
- **Modals**: Centered with smooth animations

### Animations
- **Entrance**: Fade and scale animations
- **Hover**: Scale and color transitions
- **Loading**: Rotating spinner
- **Transitions**: Smooth 300ms duration
